Obtaining permits for an accessory dwelling unit can be a complex process that requires careful planning, accurate documentation, and compliance with local regulations. ADU King Build provides professional ADU permit services for homeowners seeking custom ADUs, garage conversions, detached ADUs, backyard cottages, and junior ADUs throughout Los Angeles and surrounding communities.

Our permit specialists coordinate every stage of the approval process, including zoning reviews, plan submissions, building code compliance, and communication with local agencies.
